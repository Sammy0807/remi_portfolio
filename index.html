About Me

Remi Olagoke is a Nigerian-American model, actress, dancer and Speech-Language Pathologist. Remi was raised in Raleigh, North Carolina but is currently based in Houston, Texas. Remi first got her start in modeling at John Casablancas Professional Modeling Center. Since then, she used her talents to engage in various promotional modeling and runway shows. Remi has always had a passion for creativity, innovation and fashion. Watching shows like America’s Next Top Model and Project Runway inspired her to lead a new wave of faces in fashion. At that time, she saw the limited availability of plus-sized African-American models in high fashion as well as mainstream media and now aspires to be the change she wants to see in the fashion world. As a young plus sized/curve model, Remi faced some challenges due to lack of size range in the fashion world that typically caters to standard model sizes. In her pursuits to collaborate with brands, she often found herself faced with the challenge of “not being their type” or being pushed aside due the client/designer not having anything for her to model. In her past modeling agency, she felt that she was not submitted for work as often her counterparts due to her look. She knew that the opportunities in her region were limited and if she wanted to progress in this industry, she would need to move to a more diverse region that caters to her personal brand. This propelled her to move to a centralized state like Texas. Remi obtained her bachelor’s degree in Speech-Language Pathology from The University of North Carolina at Greensboro and her master’s degree in Speech-Language Pathology from The Hampton University. Aside from modeling, Remi works as a full time Speech-Language Pathologist (SLP) in a pediatric medical clinic. In her career, she works with children to improve their communication difficulties so they can have a voice. Aside from modeling, Remi has also been acting since she was 14 and often seeks professional opportunities to further her craft. In her free time, Remi enjoys traveling, festivals, taking dance classes, thrifting, visiting museums, attending plays and trying local food. She is the Co-Founder of her foodie blog, “The Savory Sistas” (@thesavorysista on instagram). One beauty habit Remi abides by is removing all makeup and washing her face before bed each night. On a daily basis Remi refrains from wearing makeup and follows a simple skin care regimen of natural products including black soap, rose water, witch hazel, vitamin E and lemon oil. As her career continues to progress, whether through modeling, acting, dance or healthcare, Remi Olagoke is definitely one to watch!
